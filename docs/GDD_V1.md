# GDD v1 (Concise)

## 1. Product Statement
A browser-first, live-ops strategy MMO with city building, hero progression, tile/lane combat, alliance warfare, and 8–12 week seasonal loops.

## 2. Audience
- Primary: strategy/F2P MMO players (mobile-native behaviors) who want short daily sessions and long-term alliance goals.
- Secondary: desktop strategy players seeking persistent social metagame.

## 3. Core Loops
### Micro (2–5 min)
Collect resources, run one battle, claim rewards, refresh queues.

### Mid (15–60 min)
Campaign progression, hero upgrades, alliance contributions/rallies.

### Macro (weekly/seasonal)
Territory control, faction conflict, seasonal leaderboard progression with partial resets.

## 4. Vertical Slice Content Envelope
- Buildings: 10
- Heroes: 12
- Enemy factions: 3
- Battle modes: campaign + map skirmish
- Alliance: basic donations + rally join

## 5. Success Metrics (Slice)
- D1 tutorial completion: >= 65%
- D1 retention proxy (24h return in test cohort): >= 35%
- Avg first-session length: 18–30 min
- Battle crash-free rate: >= 99.5%

## 6. Economy (Slice)
- Soft currencies: food, metal, energy
- Hard currency: gems
- Sinks: building upgrades, hero level/ascension, speedups
- Sources: tasks, map nodes, campaign rewards

## 7. Non-goals (Slice)
- No full server-vs-server season.
- No advanced alliance diplomacy tooling.
- No deep cosmetic store catalog.

## 8. Risks and Controls
- Scope creep: freeze slice scope with formal change requests.
- Combat readability: enforce minimum VFX clarity and combat log output.
- Performance on mobile web: cap atlas size and dynamic effects.
